About Rockenue

We built the infrastructure independent hotels were missing.

"We don't take over the hotel. We take over everything behind it — and leave the identity untouched."

— James Whitmore, Founder & CEO

Founded in London, 2019. We operate independent hotels the way a chain does — pricing, distribution, technology, and commercial strategy managed centrally — while every hotel keeps its own name.

Independents weren't losing on quality. They were losing on infrastructure: chains had better systems, better deals, more data. We built what was missing — proprietary AI pricing, a live market-intelligence layer, and chain-level distribution — and made it available to hotels that would otherwise go without.
